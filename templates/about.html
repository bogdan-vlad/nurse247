Who we are and
what we do

Your Nurse 24/7 is a company set up to service the needs of Healthcare organisations to find suitable Nursing staff for day and night shifts. We understand that Healthcare is a 24 hour, 365 days a year business and as such requires true flexibility whereby Healthcare institutions are adequately staffed around the clock.

Our temporary staffing division understand the hardship faced by Majority of Healthcare institutions with finding Nursing staff that are willing to work day shifts whereby the preference of majority of agency Nurses tends to be for night shifts. Your Nurse 24/7 provides a Niche service whereby all temporary Nurses on our books are required to be flexible enough to accommodate both day and night shifts.

Our aim is to provide a valued and much needed service to enable our clients to continuously meet their staffing quotas. In order to achieve this we aim to provide an effective recruitment service for ad-hoc and contract placements to both Nurses and Clients.

Our Team is made up of experienced individuals who have a thorough understanding of the market and who work tirelessly to exceed our Client’s expectations.

We believe in delivering quality healthcare professionals 24/7 and this have a continuous recruitment process to ensure the compliance of our Nurses and also to guarantee client satisfaction.

We provide a nationwide service to both Clients and Nurses, supplying to:

  • NHS ACUTE TRUSTS
  • HEALTH AND CARE TRUSTS
  • PRIVATE HOSPITALS
  • NURSING & RESIDENTIAL HOMES

and many more institutions.
